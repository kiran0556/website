    Departments

    Our Departments

    CAI

    Focuses on designing intelligent systems capable of learning, reasoning, and problem-solving. Prepares students for careers in AI-driven technologies and automation.

    CIVIL

    Focuses on the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure projects such as roads, bridges, buildings, and water supply systems. Prepares students for careers in civil engineering and construction management.

    CSE

    Focuses on the design, development, and maintenance of software systems and applications. Prepares students for careers in software engineering, data science, and system design.

    ECE

    Focuses on the design and development of electronic systems, circuits, and devices. Prepares students for careers in electronics engineering, embedded systems, and telecommunications.

    EEE

    Focuses on the study and application of electrical energy, including power generation, transmission, and distribution. Prepares students for careers in electrical engineering, power systems, and renewable energy.

    MECH

    Focuses on the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems and components. Prepares students for careers in mechanical engineering, manufacturing, and product design.

    CDS

    Focuses on the design, development, and analysis of computer systems and software. Prepares students for careers in computer science, data analysis, and system architecture.

    CML

    Focuses on the study of materials and their properties, including metals, ceramics, polymers, and composites. Prepares students for careers in materials science, manufacturing, and product development.
